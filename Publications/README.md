# Zhe Wee (Derrick) Ng's Publications

Selected peer-reviewed journal and conference publications.

## Publications

### 1. Machine Learning-Aided Supply Chain Analysis of Waste Management Systems: System Optimization for Sustainable Production

- **Authors:** Zhe Wee Ng, Bidyut Debnath, and A. K. Chattopadhyay
- **Corresponding authors:** Zhe Wee Ng and A. K. Chattopadhyay
- **Venue:** *Sustainability*, 17(19), Article 8848, 2025
- **Summary:** Developed a machine-learning framework for circular e-waste supply-chain optimization, combining feedforward neural networks, random forests, and Monte Carlo simulation to address data sparsity and evaluate system resilience. The study reported predictive performance above R2 = 0.97 across 21 variables and identified economic and environmental levers influencing supply-chain sustainability.
- **Link:** [Publisher page](https://www.mdpi.com/2071-1050/17/19/8848)
- **Citation:** Ng, Z. W., Debnath, B., & Chattopadhyay, A. K. (2025). Machine learning-aided supply chain analysis of waste management systems: System optimization for sustainable production. *Sustainability*, 17(19), 8848.

### 2. Data-Driven Sustainable Design Opportunities from Automated User Insights

- **Authors:** Nikita Goridkov, Zhe Wee Ng, Fiona Chen, and Kosa Goucher-Lambert
- **Venue:** Proceedings of the ASME 2025 International Design Engineering Technical Conferences and Computers and Information in Engineering Conference (IDETC-CIE 2025), Anaheim, CA, Paper IDETC2025-169019
- **Summary:** Built a dataset of 23,000 Amazon reviews across 290 sustainability-certified products and applied BERT, VADER, LDA, NMF, and GPT-based analysis to extract design-relevant sustainability signals. The work connected user sentiment, product certifications, and recurring consumer concerns to identify sustainable-design opportunities from large-scale review data.
- **Link:** [Conference paper](https://codesign.berkeley.edu/papers/goridkov-reviews-idetc/)
- **Citation:** Goridkov, N., Ng, Z. W., Chen, F., & Goucher-Lambert, K. (2025). Data-driven sustainable design opportunities from automated user insights. *Proceedings of the ASME 2025 International Design Engineering Technical Conferences and Computers and Information in Engineering Conference*, IDETC2025-169019.
